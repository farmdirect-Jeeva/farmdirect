## 🎯 The Problem

140 million Indian farmers lose **40-60% of their income** 
to middlemen every single day.

Farmers sell tomatoes at ₹12/kg.
Middlemen sell the same tomatoes at ₹40/kg.
**Farmers get nothing. Middlemen get rich.**

This is wrong. FarmDirect fixes this.

---

## 💡 The Solution

FarmDirect is a **zero commission AgriTech marketplace** 
that connects farmers directly to consumers.

No middlemen. No commission. 
Every rupee goes directly to the farmer.

## ✨ Key Features

- ✅ **Zero Commission** — Farmers keep 100% profit
- 🌐 **Language Translation** — Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam
- 📍 **Location Based** — Shows nearest farmers first
- 💰 **Price Transparency** — Market price vs farmer price
- 🚚 **Live Delivery Tracking** — Real time GPS tracking
- 🌧️ **Climate Alerts** — Auto notifies buyers of weather delays
- 🛡️ **Trust System** — Verified farmer badges
- 📸 **Quality Verification** — Fresh photos before dispatch
